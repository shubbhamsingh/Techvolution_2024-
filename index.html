Overview of techvolution

What it was

An inaugural tech conference held in Malaysia on February 27th, 2024. Organized by SWAG Holdings Sdn Bhd, a group representing various industries.

Focus

Discussing Malaysia's progress towards its digital goals. Highlighting the digitalization of the Malaysian economy. Exploring four key tech missions for the country. Fostering collaboration for driving innovation and transformation.

Featured presentations by tech leaders from various sectors. Panel discussions on trending topics like: Electric Vehicles Artificial Intelligence (AI) The Creative Economy Globalization
